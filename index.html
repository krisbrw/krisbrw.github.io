About me

Data person.
Nashville native.

Hey! I'm Kris — a Business Intelligence Engineer and data analyst based in Nashville, TN. I love the full journey from raw data to real insight: writing the SQL, building the pipeline, designing the architecture, and making sure the right people can actually use what comes out the other end.

My day-to-day lives in SQL, Python, and AWS — building ETL pipelines, designing data models, and wiring up services like Glue, Lambda, Redshift, and S3 into something that just works. I care a lot about clean architecture and data that people can trust.

Right now I'm building Athenium, a Nashville data platform that ingests local open data and exposes it as a commercial product, and Kiwi-Finance, a personal finance app with real-time transaction tracking powered by Plaid.
